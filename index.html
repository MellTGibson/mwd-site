A little bit about me

I have a little confession to make, I’m a self-confessed Tech Geek! I love gadgets and all things tech related. A Canadian by birth I currently live in London England where I call home with my very supportive husband, and our little furbaby, Harlequin.

My background has mainly been in Human Resources within Corporate Finance but I’ve always loved the technical aspects of my job. Any excuse to create something techie I was all over it! Whether it would be an Excel database or a SharePoint site. My journey into code started when I took a foundations course in Mobile App Development. It was there I was introduced to a bit of HTML and from then on I was hooked on code!

I’m now pursuing my passion as a Front End Web Developer utilising my organisational skills and attention to detail where I can help you create a website that suits your specific needs and creativity. Whether that's a bespoke website, or logo creation or you simply don't know where to start but you know you need to be on the web, I'm here to help.

How can I help you?

Specialties

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organised CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Convert Desings into Code

I can create high fidelity wireframes so you can see how your website will look like before we start. Have a designer's PSD already, no problem, I can quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.

Responsive Design

Do you currently have a website you love and looks great on a desktop but not on your mobile or tablet? I can convert a static site to a responsive site so it's optimised for mobile devices.

Content Management Systems

Fancy a website you can update yourself? I can design and build a website for you in a CMS then teach you, how to update it yourself.

Social Media

Need help with building your social media platforms such as a Facebook Business page? I can help you create business social media platforms that are linked to your website.
